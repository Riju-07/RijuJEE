RIJU-JEE

Riju Joint Entrance Examination (RJEE) is a national-level entrance test designed to identify and nurture the brightest engineering aspirants across India. Conducted annually, RJEE emphasizes conceptual clarity, logical reasoning, and innovation over rote memorization.

Key Features of RJEE:

    
🧠 Focus Areas: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Applied Logic

🧪 Special Round: “Innovation Aptitude Test” to assess problem-solving in real-world tech scenarios

🏆 Top 1% Advantage: Direct admission to premier institutes under the Riju Institute of Emerging Technologies (RIET)

💡 Scholarships: Full tuition waiver + innovation grants for top performers  

📅 Mode: Hybrid (online + offline centers) with a flexible slot system

🌐 Syllabus: Based on NCERT, but with an added emphasis on creative application and AI-readiness


RJEE aims to revolutionize how engineering potential is measured—less about marks, more about mindset.
